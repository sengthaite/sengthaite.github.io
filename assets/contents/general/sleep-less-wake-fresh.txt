### Efficient Sleep Formula

1. **Consistent Sleep Schedule**: Sleep and waking at the same time every day.
2. **90-Minutes Rule**: Plan sleep in multiples of 90mn (e.g. 4.5h, 6h, or 7.5h)
3. **Pre-Sleep Shutdown Sequence**: 3-2-1 method (60mn) to power down. 3h no heavy meals, 2h no work or stress, 1h no screen
4. **Morning Light Exposure**: Step outside for 10mn of sunlight afer waking
5. **Cold Water or Movement**: Washing your face with cold water or doing light exercise after waking

### Advanced Sleep Optimization Tricks

1. **Caffeine Timing**: no caffeine after 2pm
2. **Ideal Sleep Environment**: Ensure room is dark, cold (18-20 Degree Celsius) and quite
3. **Magnesium and Herbal Support**: calm nervous system
4. **Mental Declutter**: Dump your thought on paper before bed
